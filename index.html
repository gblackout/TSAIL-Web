Tsinghua SAIL Group

Statistical Artificial Intelligence & Learning

Research Overview

We are interested in developing machine learning theories, algorithms, and applications to problems in science, engineering and computing. We use the tools of statistical (in particular Bayesian) inference to deal with uncertainty and information in various domains, including text mining, image & video processing, network analysis, web data mining, and neuroscience.

We actively seek to collaborate with other groups around the world. If you are interested in finding out more about our research, please visit our publication page.
