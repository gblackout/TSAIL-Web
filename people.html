Principal Investigator

Jun Zhu

Associate Professor at Tsinghua University, Adjunct Faculty at Carnegie Mellon University

I am an Associate Professor of Computer Science at Tsinghua University, and an Adjunct Faculty of Machine Learning at Carnegie Mellon University. My research focuses on developing statistical machine learning methods to understand complex scientific and engineering data. My current interests are in latent variable models, large-margin learning, Bayesian nonparametrics, and sparse learning in high dimensions. Before joining Tsinghua in 2011, I was a post-doc researcher and project scientist at the Machine Learning Department in Carnegie Mellon University.
